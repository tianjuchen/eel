ADNeumannBC

Imposes the integrated boundary condition , where is a constant, controllable value.

Description

ADNeumannBC is the simplest type of ADIntegratedBC, and is used for imposing flux boundary conditions on systems of partial differential equations (PDEs). This class is appropriate to use for PDEs of the form where is the domain, and is its boundary. In this case, a ADNeumannBC object is used to impose the condition (3) on the subset of the boundary denoted by . The value parameter corresponds to the constant , and the user must define one or more sidesets corresponding to the boundary subset .
